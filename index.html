About BlockTribe

BlockTribe is a community of engineers, managers, entrepreneurs who believe in the power of decentralisation and wish to learn, share or teach about existing and upcoming distributed ledger technologies and its applications in the real world. Note - BlockTribe is not a platform to discuss cryptocurrencies or share investment ideas in the crypto world. Just the technology and its applications.

BlockTribe meets every Saturday, and each meetup is divided into 3 sessions. In first session of 2 hours, we break down a white paper to the audience leaving no detail untouched, followed with a 15 minute Q&A. In the last session, people from the audience can present something they have been working on in this space which helps them connect with like-minded blockers.
